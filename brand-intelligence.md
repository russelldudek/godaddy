## Official company sources
- https://www.godaddy.com/

## Official identity asset
- Source: official GoDaddy careers page asset served from `files.clinchtalent.com`.
- Local path: `assets/brand/godaddy-official.svg`.
- Decision: use the unmodified black GO symbol and GoDaddy wordmark above the fold and in restrained document headers for nominative employer identification.
- Independent distinction: every surface identifies the work as an independent candidate vision by Russell Dudek and disclaims endorsement.

## Color token provenance

- `#09757a` teal
- `#00a4a6` aqua
- `#004249` deep teal
- `#41db00` lime
- `#ffdb00` yellow
- `#ff5b5b` coral

Candidate-original supporting colors are limited to pale mint and pale blue backgrounds and remain subordinate.

## Typography evidence and decision
Official careers CSS names `GDSage` and `GDSherpa`, with Helvetica and Arial fallbacks. Proprietary font binaries are not copied. The campaign uses Trebuchet MS/Avenir-like system fallbacks for rounded humanist display behavior and Helvetica/Arial-compatible body text. The substitute is not described as official.

## Visual grammar
- bold black type and generous white space;
- bright, confident accent blocks;
- rounded product-friendly surfaces;
- direct, human, entrepreneurial language;
- visible customer agency rather than autonomous-system spectacle.

## Original role-derived art direction
The candidate-created Entrepreneur Outcome Resolver translates domain-resolution discipline into an applied-AI decision contract: STATE, NEXT, AUTH, PROOF, and TTL. The visual uses a resolver record, address/query grammar, and timed re-resolution. It does not reproduce a GoDaddy interface or claim to describe internal systems.

## Imagery decision
Official careers photography was captured during research but is not used in the final campaign. The role argument is better served by an original system visual; avoiding employee photography also reduces the risk of implying organizational knowledge or authorship. Company recognition comes from the official identity, palette, typography behavior, and product/domain grammar.

## Artifact use
- Site: full palette, official lockup, resolver visual, role-derived motion and interaction.
- Resume/cover letter: restrained official wordmark, teal rule, shared header.
- Brief, plan, objection analysis, questions, and resolver PDF: same header, rules, callouts, and color grammar.
