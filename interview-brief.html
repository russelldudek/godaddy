Mandate beneath the title

Build a globally scalable applied AI personalization system that connects customer context, agentic action, end-to-end ML pipelines, rapid experimentation, governance, and team execution to observable entrepreneur growth.

Company moment

What GoDaddy has already established

  • A broad entrepreneur platform spanning domains, sites, marketing, commerce, security, and support.
  • Airo and Airo AI Builder as increasingly capable AI-powered creation and business tools.
  • A public philosophy that AI should reduce friction while the customer retains control of the brand.
  • An innovation model focused on moving processes from human-powered to structured, automated, and data-powered.

Why the role matters now

  • The posting asks for a three-year roadmap, not an isolated feature plan.
  • Personalized applications must work across products, regions, and customer bases.
  • Agentic AI raises the authority, trust, observability, and operating-model stakes.
  • Rapid experimentation must compound into reusable platform capability and business value.

Decision architecture

Fund

Shared capabilities with demonstrated reuse across multiple entrepreneur journeys.

Scale

Experiments with outcome evidence, production ownership, governance, and adoption.

Hold or stop

Work with weak relevance, unclear authority, poor economics, or unreliable evidence.

Standardize

Evaluation, observability, authority contracts, regional controls, and release criteria.

Localize

Language, channel, product, regulation, and customer-maturity decisions.

Escalate

Sensitive exceptions, ambiguous authority, trust risk, and low-confidence decisions.
