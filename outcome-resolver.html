Entrepreneur Outcome Resolver

A contract for trustworthy next-best-move personalization. Illustrative - not a description of GoDaddy's internal architecture.

Purpose

Translate a customer's changing business context into one useful next move while making agent authority, evidence, and freshness explicit. The Resolver is designed to improve product, engineering, analytics, Guide, governance, and executive conversations about applied AI.

The resolution record

STATECurrent intent, business maturity, product state, observed behavior, constraints, uncertainty, and data freshness.input
NEXTOne action likely to advance a named entrepreneur outcome now.decision
AUTHDraft, recommend, execute within explicit bounds, or escalate to customer/Guide/owner.control
PROOFCustomer movement, relevance, trust, quality, economics, latency, operating load, and regional compliance.evidence
TTLTime or event that invalidates the resolution and requires updated context.learning

Resolution rules

  1. No NEXT without a named customer outcome.
  2. No action without an explicit AUTH boundary and owner.
  3. No continuation without comparable PROOF.
  4. No confidence without a freshness horizon.
  5. No global reuse without local policy and context review.
  6. No production scale without observability, rollback, and recovery.

Illustrative default

STATE: Site is live; discoverability is weak. NEXT: Diagnose intent gaps and prepare a focused acquisition experiment. AUTH: Recommend and prepare; entrepreneur approves publishing and spend. PROOF: Qualified visits, engaged sessions, conversion event, cost guardrail. TTL: 7 days.

Disposition

RESOLVE

Context is sufficient, authority is clear, and evidence/rollback are ready.

BOUND

Proceed with narrower authority, audience, channel, budget, or duration.

ESCALATE

Human judgment is required because authority, sensitivity, confidence, or consequence is unclear.

REFRESH

State is stale or changed; do not reuse the prior answer.

HOLD

Evidence or production ownership is incomplete.

STOP

Customer value, trust, quality, economics, or compliance does not justify continuation.

Portfolio use

Each roadmap initiative should produce a set of comparable resolution records. That creates a portfolio view of customer moments, shared capabilities, unresolved authority, evidence strength, local variants, and reusable components - without relying on an unsupported maturity score.

Leadership cadence

  • Weekly: experiment and production resolution review.
  • Monthly: shared capability, economics, incident, and regional-variant review.
  • Quarterly: roadmap investment, scale, hold, stop, and team-capability decisions.
