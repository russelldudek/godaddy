Executive interview questions

Questions that test the operating mandate.

Each question is designed to surface a decision, dependency, or tension rather than invite rehearsed company description.

1. Which entrepreneur outcome most needs personalization to change in the next 18 months?

Reveals the priority customer moment and business consequence behind the roadmap.

2. Where is agent authority currently most ambiguous?

Clarifies when systems draft, recommend, act, or escalate to customers and Guides.

3. What must remain globally consistent, and what must vary by market, product, or customer context?

Surfaces regional, data, policy, language, and operating boundaries.

4. Which promising experiments have not become durable platform capabilities - and why?

Tests handoff, ownership, observability, economics, and reuse.

5. How do product teams consume shared AI/ML capabilities without creating a central bottleneck?

Clarifies platform versus domain ownership and the service model of the central team.

6. What evidence causes GoDaddy to increase investment, narrow scope, hold, or stop an AI initiative?

Reveals the actual portfolio decision standard.

7. How do GoDaddy Guides participate in a future where customer-facing agents can perceive, decide, and act?

Connects human expertise, escalation, coaching, and customer trust.

8. What is the hardest technical or organizational dependency in the three-year roadmap?

Surfaces the real sequencing constraint.

9. How will success be distinguished from engagement that does not create customer momentum?

Tests measurement quality and outcome alignment.

10. What is the strongest reason this hire could fail despite excellent AI/ML credentials?

Invites candid discussion of adoption, alignment, leadership, and operating burden.
